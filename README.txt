== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What is Winston AI? =
Winston AI is the leading AI content detector, helping millions of users detect use of ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude and much more. 

= What is the website AI scanner? =
The website AI scanner allows any Wordpress site owner to scan each of their blog articles for AI content. Each article is scanned, individually, and gets an assessment (0-100%) of the odds of each article being written by a human or AI. 

= What is the HUMN-1 Certification? =
The HUMN-1 certification is an audit performed by Winston AI to assess if a website’s content is written by humans or AI. If our audit, performed on random pages-articles published on your website, passes our AI detection guidelines, you will be awarded a certificate of human content to display on your website and articles. 

= What are the guidelines for obtaining a HUMN-1 or HUMN-1+ certificate? =
To get a HUMN-1 certificate, Winston AI will scan up to 10 random pages of your website every month. 70% or more of the content scanned should be considered human to get a certificate. For HUMN-1+, 30 articles are scanned monthly.

= What is the HUMN-1 Certification for? =
The certification is built to help any website owners wishing to stand out in a sea of synthetic content online. Stand out from the crowd of AI sites and see your rankings on search engines jump.
